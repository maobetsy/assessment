Dictionary

How to use?

This project serves as a dictionary tool or application. To use this simple application, you can begin by typing a search term or word into the input field. When you submit your search term, the results will be returned from an API. The results will display the word's definition and part of speech.

This project utilises the Free Dictionary API.

The search field only accepts letters. Spaces and hyphens are only accepted if you are searching for open or hyphenated compound words. Numbers, symbols and repeating spaces or hyphens are not accepted.
